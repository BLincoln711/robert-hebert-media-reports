Forecast Assumptions

Industry benchmarks and market data used in this forecast

Average CPC: $14-$18

Louisiana market is less competitive than major metros, allowing for lower cost-per-click on "we buy houses" keywords.

Conversion Rate: 6-8%

Based on optimized landing page with clear CTA. Industry average is 3-5% but your site structure supports higher rates.

Lead-to-Deal: 7-10%

Industry standard for cash buyer leads is 1-in-10 to 1-in-15 motivated seller leads resulting in a closed deal.

Avg Deal Profit: $10-15K

Based on Louisiana market wholesale/assignment fees. National average ranges $5K-$30K depending on deal type.

Geographic: Louisiana

Targeting Louisiana market with focus on high-intent keywords for motivated sellers in distressed situations.

Ramp Period: 60-90 Days

Google Ads requires 2-3 months to optimize. Full performance expected after learning period completes.

Strategic Recommendations

Maximize ROI with these proven strategies

Start with Long-Tail Keywords

Target specific phrases like "sell inherited house fast Louisiana" for lower CPCs and higher-intent leads.

Dedicated Landing Pages

Create specific pages for each keyword theme (foreclosure, divorce, probate) to boost conversion rates.

Implement Call Tracking

Track phone calls as conversions - many motivated sellers prefer calling over forms. Essential for accurate ROI.

Commit to 90 Days Minimum

Google's algorithm needs time to learn. Expect full performance after the initial optimization period.
