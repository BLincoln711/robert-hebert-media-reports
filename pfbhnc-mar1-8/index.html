Key Insights
✓
General Psychiatry Campaign Delivering Strong Savings

The General Psychiatry & Counseling PMax campaign—the largest by spend—reduced cost by 18.3% this week while maintaining a stellar 19.79% CTR and keeping CPC at just $0.53. This campaign continues to be the most efficient driver of qualified traffic in the account.

✓
Spravato Leads PMax Growing Reach

Spravato Leads PMax delivered a 27.5% increase in impressions (2,083 vs 1,634) and 20.3% more clicks (308 vs 256) while keeping spend essentially flat (-1.15%). CPC improved significantly from $0.99 to $0.82 (down 17.8%). This campaign is scaling efficiently.

✓
Therapy Services Charlotte Expanding Visibility

The Charlotte Therapy Services search campaign saw a 37.7% surge in impressions (785 vs 570) and 14% more clicks (73 vs 64). While spend increased 23.2%, this reflects growing demand capture in the Charlotte market at a competitive $2.03 CPC—the lowest among Search campaigns.

!
Click Volume Declined Across PMax Campaigns

Overall clicks dropped 9.3% week-over-week, primarily driven by the General Psychiatry campaign (-467 clicks, -17.9%). This coincides with the spend reduction and may reflect natural budget pacing. The NewADHD PMax campaign partially offset this with 11% more impressions and steady click growth (+5.2%).
