Key Insights & Analysis

More Clicks, Less Cost Per Click

The campaign achieved 128 more clicks while reducing average CPC by $0.20. This indicates improved ad relevance and quality score, allowing the campaign to win more auctions at lower costs.

CTR Shows Strong Ad Resonance

A 6.52% CTR is excellent for Search campaigns. The 15% improvement suggests ad copy and targeting are well-aligned with searcher intent for pre-owned designer eyewear.

Impression Decline Worth Monitoring

While impressions dipped 3.4%, the higher CTR and lower CPC suggest Google is showing ads to more qualified audiences. Quality over quantity is winning here.

Budget Efficiency Maximized

Spending essentially the same amount while generating 11% more traffic represents significant efficiency gains. The campaign is getting more value per dollar spent.
